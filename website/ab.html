HELLO !!

Photography is something through which I have grown, I have explored my imaginations and creativity. I consider expressing one's ideas and emotions through the art of photography absolutely surreal.

I remember those times when I used to capture anything and everything that used to catch my eye in an aesthetic mood. Born and brought up in Kolkata, I started capturing moments with my mobile's camera the day I found beauty in the streets of the City of joy and an untold story in those mysteryladen lanes of North Kolkata.

Falling in love with the streets,trying to figure out an essence through those and hence depicting it through photographs was the start to it. Henceforth I also shifted my area of interests from streets to landscape,architectures,minimalism,street portraits and sometimes also abstract.

Documenting your experience,jotting them down on a piece of paper,is definitely one of the possible ways to reciprocate one's views as a traveller. But, visuals have been communicating better than words since time immemorial. "A picture paints a thousand words".
